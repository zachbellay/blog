## Ingredients:

- Elderflower Liquer ~ 2 shots
    - On a budget: Bols Elderflower Liqueur 750mL ($12-$18)
    - Ballin' out: Saint Germain Elderflower Liquer 750mL ($38-$52)
- Gin ~ 2 shots
- 4 Lemons
- Ice
- Prosecco 
- 4 Champagne flutes
- Simple syrup
